    Meet Ella

    I have always loved making art, even when I got bullied for it and when my surroundings told me that it was a dumb idea. I'm here to prove them wrong.

    Binjour, nice to meet you. I'am Ella and I'm 21 years old. I have been a fantasy manipulation artist since I was 15. And even earlier, when I took that crayon and showed him who is the boss! I adore fantasy manipulations, illustrations and to draw traditionally

    I believe in

    “They say that if you dream, a thing more then once, it’s bound to come true”

    Since I was 15, my vision was to make art and share it with a lot of people. I wanted to inspire and give hope to people. And that is exactly my vision today as well. I want to create a big loving community, a safe haven to new and existing members. A community where we share our art and talk about a bunch of topics. Where everyone is accepting and supportive.

    Lost Childhood

    Meaning

    Most of my works have a story behind them. For example, "Lost Childhood" is about how my childhood got sweapt away within a blink from my hands and now that I'm looking back, I remember how innocent and beautiful it was and how the people around me like my grandparents made it ten times better. Some works I make for fun and because the idea sounded fabulous in my head.
